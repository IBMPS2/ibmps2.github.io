Market and Craft Shops

At Chollerton Tea rooms we provide both a General and Craft shop for all of your needs at Chollerton, you are never too far away from the services you need!

Our shops provide various services to the people of Chollerton and people visiting. Our general stores will help you keep going with the items you need at affordable prices in order to make your stay here as easy and relaxed as possible

Our craft shops are always here to keep you busy if that is your sort of thing!

Our shops are open from 8am to 10am all week! This is true for most of our services including our tearoom, post office and bed & Breakfast.

If you would like to find out more about our services, please click on the links below to the service you want. Is what you are looking for not there? Then send us a query by going to the find out more page and filling out a form!
