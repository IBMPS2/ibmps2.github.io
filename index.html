Welcome to Chollerton Tea Rooms

Welcome, we are a company based in the heart of Tyne Valley in the village of Chollerton, since 1931 we have been providing the village and those visiting with our services and holidays including a tea room, B&B, post office and our local village store.

Our traditional tea room has been the heart of the village since 1931, providing local and international assortments of tea, coffee, cakes, and biscuits. Our services have been widely regarded as the best for providing the best possible experience and services with our tea room and have subsequently stolen the hearts of the people of Chollerton.

For those wanting to stay overnight in Chollerton or spend a holiday in our beautiful village, we have the perfect solution for your accommodation. We provide a highly rated Bed & breakfast service for you're every day needs when staying at Chollerton. Our B&B service is the best solution for accommodation for your holiday at Chollerton.

At Chollerton Tea rooms we provide both a General and Craft shop for all of your needs at Chollerton, you are never too far away from the services you need!

Need to post something whilst you stay at Chollerton? A letter? A Postcard? then you will be in delight to hear that we provide a post office in the village of Chollerton. We offer a privatized post office which has been the main post office in Chollerton for many years and has satisfied the local residents since its creation.
